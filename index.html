The Backstory

Born in a Garage. Still Going.

Garage Pizza is an Italian-style thin crust pizza restaurant at 196 Pittwater Road in Manly, on Sydney's Northern Beaches, established in 2004. It opened with one oven, a handful of tables, and a dead-simple idea: make proper thin crust pizza, use real ingredients, and don't overcomplicate it. Two decades later, that hasn't changed.

Every afternoon at five, the dough comes out. It's made fresh that day — stretched by hand, never rolled, never frozen. Our tomato sauce is cooked from scratch in the same kitchen you can see from your seat. The produce is local and seasonal — silverbeet from the markets, tomatoes picked that week — because that's what tastes best. Whether you're after a classic pepperoni or a vegetarian pizza in Manly, we've got you covered.

The seating is all outdoors — pallet tables, metal stools, the sound of Pittwater Road on a warm evening. In winter, we crank up the heaters and you can watch your breath mix with the steam off a fresh Supremo. Grab a cold beer. Let the dog sit at your feet. Nobody's rushing you.

Some of our regulars have been coming since the beginning — twenty years of the same order, the same table. Their kids grew up eating our Margheritas. That kind of loyalty isn't something you manufacture. You earn it one pizza at a time.

The Menu

Simple Menu. Serious Pizza.

Garage Pizza Manly serves classic pizzas from $8 and gourmet pizzas up to $16, plus sides, desserts, beer and wine. Every pizza is made with handmade dough, homemade tomato sauce, mozzarella, and oregano.

All pizzas come with our homemade tomato base, mozzarella & oregano.

Good Question

Everything You Need to Know

Where is Garage Pizza located?

+

We're at Shop 2, 196 Pittwater Rd, Manly NSW 2095 — right in the heart of Manly on Sydney's Northern Beaches. We're just a short stroll from Manly Beach, Manly Wharf, and The Corso. You can't miss us!

What are your opening hours?

+

We're open 5pm to 9pm, seven days a week. Rain or shine, we're here rolling out dough and firing up pizzas every single evening.

What type of pizza do you serve?

+

We make Italian-style thin crust pizza with handmade dough and our own homemade tomato sauce. Every pizza comes on a thin, crispy base with mozzarella and oregano. We've got classics from $8 like the Margherita and Pepperoni, and gourmet options like the Italiano and Supremo up to $16.

Do you have vegetarian options?

+

Absolutely! We're vegetarian friendly. Our Margherita ($8) is a beautiful classic with fresh tomato, mozzarella and basil. We can also customise other pizzas to suit — just let us know when you order.

Do you do delivery?

+

We do! You can find us on UberEats and DoorDash. There's a minimum order of $18 and a $2 delivery charge. Or if you'd rather, give us a ring on (02) 8966 9234 and pick up in person — the pizza's always best straight out of the oven.

How do I order for delivery?

+

Just search for "Garage Pizza Manly" on UberEats or DoorDash and you'll find us. Pick your pizzas, check out, and we'll have them on their way. Minimum order is $18 with a $2 delivery charge. Easy as.

Is Garage Pizza family-friendly?

+

One hundred percent. We love seeing families come through. With classic pizzas starting at just $8, it's easy to feed the whole crew without breaking the bank. The kids love watching the pizzas get made, and the outdoor vibe keeps everyone relaxed.

Do you have outdoor seating?

+

All our dining is outdoors — it's part of the charm! We've got rustic pallet tables with metal stools, and in winter we set up heaters so you stay warm while you eat. It's the perfect laid-back Manly vibe.

Is there parking nearby?

+

Street parking on Pittwater Road is your best bet, or the Whistler Street car park is just a five-minute walk. Coming by ferry or bus? We're an easy stroll from Manly Wharf.

Do you accept credit cards?

+

We're cash only — old school, we know! There are ATMs nearby on The Corso and along Pittwater Road, so it's easy to grab cash on your way. It helps us keep our prices low and our pizza affordable.

Can I bring my dog?

+

Yes! We're dog-friendly and we love having furry visitors. Since all our seating is outdoors, your pup is more than welcome to join you at the table. Just don't let them steal your pepperoni.

What's your most popular pizza?

+

The Supremo and the Pepperoni are neck and neck for the top spot. The Supremo ($15) is absolutely loaded — capsicum, onion, mushroom, olives, pepperoni and ham. And our classic Pepperoni ($8) is the ultimate thin-crust crowd-pleaser. You really can't go wrong with either.

Is Garage Pizza BYO?

+

No, we're fully licensed so we can't do BYO, but we've got a great selection of beers and house wine from $8. Grab a cold one with your pizza — it's the perfect combo.

How much does pizza cost at Garage Pizza?

+

Our classic pizzas start from just $8 for a Margherita or Pepperoni, and gourmet pizzas like the Italiano and Supremo are $15–$16. Sides start at $6, and we've got beers and wine for $8. One of the most affordable spots in Manly.

Can I book a table at Garage Pizza?

+

We don't take reservations — it's walk-in only. But don't stress, we turn tables pretty quickly, especially on quieter weeknights. If it's busy, grab a beer while you wait.

Does Garage Pizza have gluten-free pizza?

+

We don't offer a gluten-free base at the moment, sorry! All our dough is made fresh with regular flour. If you've got other dietary needs, give us a ring and we'll see what we can do.

Is Garage Pizza open late?

+

We're open until 9pm seven days a week. Not a late-night spot, but we're the perfect dinner destination from 5pm. Get in early for the best seats!
