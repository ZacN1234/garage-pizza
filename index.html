The Backstory

Born in a Garage. Still Going.

Garage Pizza is an Italian-style thin crust pizza restaurant at 2/196 Pittwater Road in Manly, on Sydney's Northern Beaches, established in 2004. The idea has always been dead simple: make proper thin crust pizza, use real ingredients, and don't overcomplicate it. Two decades later, that hasn't changed.

The dough is made fresh and stretched by hand. Our tomato sauce is cooked from scratch. The produce is local and seasonal where possible — because that's what tastes best. Whether you're after a classic pepperoni or a vegetarian pizza in Manly, we've got you covered.

The seating is all outdoors — pallet tables, metal stools, the sound of Pittwater Road on a warm evening. In winter, we crank up the heaters and you can watch your breath mix with the steam off a fresh Supremo. Grab a cold beer. Let the dog sit at your feet. Nobody's rushing you.

Some of our regulars have been coming for years. As one reviewer put it, "For 20 years, Garage Pizza has been our family's go-to spot." That kind of loyalty isn't something you manufacture. You earn it one pizza at a time.

Good Question

Everything You Need to Know

Where is Garage Pizza located?

+

We're at 2/196 Pittwater Rd, Manly NSW 2095 — right in the heart of Manly on Sydney's Northern Beaches. We're just a short stroll from Manly Beach, Manly Wharf, and The Corso. You can't miss us!

What are your opening hours?

+

We're open 5pm to 9pm, Wednesday to Sunday. We're closed Monday and Tuesday. Rain or shine, we're here rolling out dough and firing up pizzas every evening we're open.

What type of pizza do you serve?

+

We make Italian-style thin crust pizza with handmade dough and our own homemade tomato sauce. Every pizza comes on a thin, crispy base with mozzarella and oregano. We do four sizes — Small, Medium, Large, and Family — with pizzas starting from just $6. We've also got pasta, garlic bread, and salads.

Do you have vegetarian options?

+

Absolutely! We've got a whole vegetarian section on the menu — the Veggie, Roma, Deli, and Jumbo Veg. Plus the Margherita and Bruschetta are meat-free too. Plenty of options!

Do you do delivery?

+

We do! You can find us on UberEats and DoorDash. There's a minimum order of $18 and a $2 delivery charge. Or if you'd rather, give us a ring on (02) 8966 9234 and pick up in person — the pizza's always best straight out of the oven.

How do I order for delivery?

+

Just search for "Garage Pizza Manly" on UberEats or DoorDash and you'll find us. Pick your pizzas, check out, and we'll have them on their way. Minimum order is $18 with a $2 delivery charge. Easy as.

Is Garage Pizza family-friendly?

+

One hundred percent. We love seeing families come through. With pizzas starting from just $6 for a small, and Family-size pizzas available, it's easy to feed the whole crew without breaking the bank. The kids love watching the pizzas get made, and the outdoor vibe keeps everyone relaxed.

Do you have outdoor seating?

+

All our dining is outdoors — it's part of the charm! We've got rustic pallet tables with metal stools, and in winter we set up heaters so you stay warm while you eat. It's the perfect laid-back Manly vibe.

Is there parking nearby?

+

Street parking on Pittwater Road is your best bet, or the Whistler Street car park is just a five-minute walk. Coming by ferry or bus? We're an easy stroll from Manly Wharf.

Do you accept card payments?

+

Yes! We accept both card and cash payments. Tap and go, credit, debit — whatever works for you.

Can I bring my dog?

+

Yes! We're dog-friendly and we love having furry visitors. Since all our seating is outdoors, your pup is more than welcome to join you at the table. Just don't let them steal your pepperoni.

What's your most popular pizza?

+

The Supremo and the Pepperoni are always popular. The Supremo ($14–$28 depending on size) is loaded — pepperoni, cabanossi, ham, mushroom, onion, capsicum, olive. And our Margherita ($7–$21) is the classic crowd-pleaser. If you're feeling adventurous, the Cosmo with prawns and pepperoni is a cracker.

Is Garage Pizza BYO?

+

No, we're not BYO, but we've got beers and wine available. Grab a cold one with your pizza — it's the perfect combo.

How much does pizza cost at Garage Pizza?

+

Our pizzas start from just $6 for a small Cheese pizza. Traditional pizzas range from $7–$14 for a small, and gourmet pizzas are $15–$16 for a small. We do four sizes — Small, Medium, Large, and Family. We've also got pasta from $12–$16, garlic bread for $5, and garden salad for $10.

Can I book a table at Garage Pizza?

+

We don't take reservations — it's walk-in only. But don't stress, we turn tables pretty quickly, especially on quieter weeknights. If it's busy, grab a beer while you wait.

Does Garage Pizza have gluten-free pizza?

+

Yes! We offer a gluten-free base for $4 extra, available in medium size only. Just let us know when you order.

Is Garage Pizza open late?

+

We're open until 9pm, Wednesday to Sunday. We're closed Monday and Tuesday. Not a late-night spot, but we're the perfect dinner destination from 5pm. Get in early for the best seats!
