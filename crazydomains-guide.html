How to Update Your Nameservers

A quick guide for garagepizza.com.au

To get your new website live on your domain, we just need to point your domain to Cloudflare. This takes about 5 minutes and won't affect your email.

Your Current Nameservers

ns21.domaincontrol.com ns22.domaincontrol.com

Your New Nameservers

[nameserver1].ns.cloudflare.com [nameserver2].ns.cloudflare.com

Zac will provide these exact nameservers

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1 Go to crazydomains.com.au and log in to your account.
  2. 2 Click on "My Account" then "Domain Names".
  3. 3 Click on garagepizza.com.au from your domain list.
  4. 4 Look for "Nameservers" or "DNS Settings" and click "Manage" or "Change".
  5. 5 Remove the current nameservers (ns21.domaincontrol.com and ns22.domaincontrol.com).
  6. 6 Enter the two new Cloudflare nameservers provided by Zac.
  7. 7 Click "Save" or "Update Nameservers".
  8. 8 That's it! Changes usually take 1 to 24 hours to take effect.

Will this affect my email?

If your email is set up through a third party (like Gmail, Outlook, or a separate email provider), changing nameservers will NOT affect your email at all.

If your email runs through Crazy Domains or GoDaddy, let Zac know first and he'll make sure everything keeps working.
