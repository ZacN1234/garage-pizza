How to Update Your Nameservers

A quick guide for garagepizza.com.au

To get your new website live on your domain, we just need to point your domain to a new hosting system (Cloudflare). This takes about 5 minutes and I'll make sure everything — including your email — keeps working perfectly.

Will this affect my email?

I'll set up all your email records (MX, SPF, DKIM) on the new system before asking you to make this change. Your email will keep working without interruption. I won't ask you to switch until everything is ready.

Current Nameservers

ns21.domaincontrol.com ns22.domaincontrol.com

New Nameservers

dave.ns.cloudflare.com shubhi.ns.cloudflare.com

Step-by-Step for Crazy Domains

1
Go to crazydomains.com.au and log in to your account.
2
Click "My Account" in the top right, then "My Domains".
3
Click on "garagepizza.com.au" from your domain list.
4
Scroll down to "Nameservers" or click the "DNS" tab.
5
Click "Modify" or "Change Nameservers".
6
Delete the current nameservers (ns21.domaincontrol.com and ns22.domaincontrol.com).
7
Enter the two new Cloudflare nameservers:
  • dave.ns.cloudflare.com
  • shubhi.ns.cloudflare.com
8
Click "Update" or "Save Changes".
Timing: Changes usually take 1–24 hours to take effect (often much faster). During this time, your website may show the old or new version intermittently — that's completely normal.
